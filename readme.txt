# DigitalGuardian - Online Harassment Detection Website

## Project Overview

DigitalGuardian is a web-based platform designed to promote safer online interactions by helping users understand, identify, and respond to online harassment, cyberbullying, hate speech, and toxic behavior. The website provides awareness resources, safety guidelines, reporting mechanisms, and educational content for creating a positive digital environment.

## Features

### AI-Powered Detection

* Analyzes online messages and content.
* Identifies potentially harmful or abusive behavior.
* Supports safer digital communication.

### Automatic Protection

* Helps users recognize harmful content.
* Encourages proactive online safety practices.

### Reporting System

* Provides mechanisms for reporting abusive behavior.
* Promotes responsible online community management.

### Support Resources

* Access to safety guidance and educational resources.
* Information on handling cyberbullying incidents.

### Community Guidelines

* Encourages respectful and inclusive online interactions.
* Promotes positive digital citizenship.

### Safe Chat Environment

* Focuses on creating secure and harassment-free communication spaces.
